A paved trail runs the length of the park along Monument Creek and connects to the larger **Pikes Peak Greenway** — you can ride or run all the way to Manitou Springs from here.

Pack a picnic, rent a bike from a nearby shop, and pop out at **Tejon Street** for coffee. Spring runoff makes the creek lively; late summer is best for the wildflower-filled meadows on the south end.
