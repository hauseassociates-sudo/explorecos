The **free self-guided factory tour** lets you watch caramels being pulled and chocolates being enrobed from a window walkway — kids love it. The **almond toffee** is the headline, but the seasonal selection (peppermint bark in winter, chocolate-covered strawberries in summer) is where the regulars stock up.

A five-minute drive from Old Colorado City or Red Rock Canyon.
