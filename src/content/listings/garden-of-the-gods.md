Start at the Visitor & Nature Center on 30th Street for the orientation film and the best view of the park before you head down. The **Perkins Central Garden Trail** is a flat 1.1-mile paved loop that weaves between the headline formations — stroller and wheelchair friendly.

Early mornings (before 9 AM) and late afternoons are magic for photos. If you can, catch sunrise from the **High Point Overlook** on Juniper Way Loop. Rain jackets in your car from May through August — afternoon thunderstorms roll in fast.
