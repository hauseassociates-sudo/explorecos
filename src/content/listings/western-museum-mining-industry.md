Time your visit for one of the **machinery demonstrations** (typically 10 AM and 1 PM) — that's when the 35-ton Corliss steam engine runs and the whole museum comes alive. The burros, **Chester and Rosebud**, are a hit with kids. Near the Air Force Academy's north gate, so easy to pair for a half-day.
