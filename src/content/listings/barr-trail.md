The trailhead is at the **top of Ruxton Avenue** in Manitou, right next to the cog railway depot. **Parking is limited and fills by 6 AM in summer** — most hikers shuttle from the **Iron Springs Chateau lot** down the hill.

For a half-day, hike up to **No Name Creek** (4 mi round-trip) or **Barr Camp** (12 mi round-trip — the traditional turnaround). The full **summit hike (26 mi round-trip)** requires either a 3 AM start or an overnight at Barr Camp.
