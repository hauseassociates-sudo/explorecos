Park at the small lot by the Starsmore Discovery Center or continue up the canyon to the Helen Hunt Falls pullout. The staircase to **Silver Cascade Falls** is short but steep — under 10 minutes to the top viewing platform. Expect mist in spring melt.

Pair with the **Mount Cutler Trail** (a few minutes down the road) to turn a quick stop into a half-day.
