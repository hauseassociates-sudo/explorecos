**About an hour southwest** via Highway 24 and Highway 67 — the drive over **Tenderfoot Hill** is half the experience. The town's **Main Street** is preserved Victorian-era, and the local herd of wild **donkeys** that roam free in summer is a real thing, not a marketing gimmick.

Don't miss the **Mollie Kathleen Gold Mine Tour** (descends 1,000 ft into the mountain) or the **Cripple Creek Heritage Center**. The casinos are mostly small-stakes and friendly — a few hours of penny slots and a chuckwagon dinner is the move.
