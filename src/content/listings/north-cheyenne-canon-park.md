Drive in from the Cheyenne Boulevard side and the road narrows into one of the prettiest scenic drives in the city — past **Helen Hunt Falls**, the Starsmore Discovery Center, and switchbacks up to **Gold Camp Road** with views back over the plains.

Stop at the **Helen Hunt Falls Visitor Center** for trail maps. Popular hikes from here include the **Mount Cutler Trail** (1.5 mi out-and-back, panoramic), **Seven Bridges** (3.6 mi, follows the creek), and the steep climb up **Mount Muscoco**. Bring layers — the canyon is shadier and ~10°F cooler than the city in summer.
