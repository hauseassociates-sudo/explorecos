**About a 45-minute drive west** through Woodland Park and Divide. The day-use parking pass (or Colorado state parks pass) is required — the entrance kiosk takes credit cards.

The **Outlook Ridge Trail** (1.2 mi to the overlook) is the easiest big-payoff hike. **Cheesman Ranch** is the longer mellow loop. Aspen viewing in late September is spectacular here — go early to beat the crowds.
