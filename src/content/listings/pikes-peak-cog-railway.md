**Book well in advance** — summer departures sell out weeks ahead. The round-trip is roughly 3 hours 20 minutes including 30–40 minutes at the summit, which is just enough time for the new visitor center and the famous donuts.

Dress warmer than you think: summit temperatures run 30–40°F colder than Manitou. Not stroller-friendly at the summit; fine for kids old enough to follow altitude rules.
