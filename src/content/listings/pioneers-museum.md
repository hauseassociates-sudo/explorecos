One of the best free museums in the city, right on **Tejon Street downtown**. The building alone is worth the visit — the **restored 1903 courtroom** is on the third floor and still has its original judge's bench.

Permanent exhibits cover the **Ute people**, **Helen Hunt Jackson**, and the founding of Colorado Springs as a "tuberculosis colony" turned resort town. Plan an hour. Pair with lunch on Tejon afterward.
