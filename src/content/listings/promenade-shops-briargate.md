The **upscale north-side shopping spot** — open-air, walkable, with a fountain plaza and outdoor fire pits in the cooler months. **Apple, Sephora, Pottery Barn, J.Crew, Williams-Sonoma** are the headliners.

Restaurants include **Brio Italian, Ted's Montana Grill, Cooper's Hawk Winery**. The **Icon Cinema** is the upscale theater option. About 20 minutes from downtown.
