Small but central — bordered by Platte, Nevada, Bijou, and Tejon. The **Acacia Park Ice Rink** runs from late November through February and is a classic Colorado Springs winter date.

Summer brings the **Acacia Park Summer Concert Series** on Wednesday and Thursday evenings (free, bring a chair). Walk a block in any direction for downtown restaurants and breweries.
