A **Manitou Avenue institution** — one of the largest old-school arcades left in the country. The vintage **mechanical games** in the front building are the gem; they still work and they still cost a penny.

Bring a roll of quarters and another of pennies. Plan an hour or three. The **Loop Restaurant** next door is the easy lunch combo.
