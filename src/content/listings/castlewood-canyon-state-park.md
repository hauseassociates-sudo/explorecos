**About an hour north** off Highway 83 — most Coloradans don't know it's here. The **Inner Canyon Loop** (1.5 mi) drops into the gorge and follows the creek past the **dam ruins**. The **Rim Rock Trail** stays up top with overlooks.

Bring water and a state-parks pass (or pay at the kiosk). The visitor center has a small natural-history exhibit. Easy add-on if you're already going to Denver for something else.
