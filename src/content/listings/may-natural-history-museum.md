Look for the **giant beetle "Hercules"** out front — that's how you know you've arrived. The museum is a true family roadside-Americana experience built around the May family's century of insect collecting around the world.

Kids love it. Adults appreciate the wonderfully not-modernized exhibits (the labels are all hand-typed). Combine with a drive up **Old Stage Road** for great views.
