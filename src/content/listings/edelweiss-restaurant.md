A **Colorado Springs institution since 1969** — the building looks like it was helicoptered in from Garmisch. Full Bavarian menu: **Wiener schnitzel**, **rouladen**, **spaetzle**, all the classic sausages.

The **biergarten patio** is open in warm months with live accordion most weekends. Reservations are smart for dinner. Bring an appetite — the portions are unapologetic.
