Named for **Paul Intemann**, the Manitou-based environmentalist who championed the trail in the 80s. You can hike any segment — the eastern Manitou trailhead is the most popular, with switchbacks up to the first ridge.

The full traverse to **Section 16** is best done as a car shuttle. Otherwise, hike **2 miles in and out** for the best view-to-effort ratio. Watch for the painted Intemann markers on rocks.
