**About 45 minutes west** via Highway 24. The **Petrified Forest Loop** (1 mi) is the classic short walk past the largest stumps. The visitor center has hands-on fossil exhibits that are surprisingly good for kids.

Pair with a stop in **Woodland Park** or **Divide** for lunch on the way home, or extend into **Mueller State Park** which is just down the road.
