Go early — the best stuff moves before 10 AM and the crowds thin out after noon. Bring cash for the outdoor stalls; most established vendors take cards. $3 parking, free admission. Covered pavilions keep part of the market running even in weather.

A half-day on its own, or an easy pre-lunch stop on your way downtown.
