You must reserve a free timed-entry pass at coloradosprings.gov before you go. Bring more water than you think you need — the altitude here starts at 6,500 feet. Most visitors descend via the **Barr Trail** (about 3 miles) rather than walking back down the steps.

Give yourself 1.5–3 hours round-trip. Park in Manitou Springs and take the free shuttle from the Iron Springs Chateau lot.
