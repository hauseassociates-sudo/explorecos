Start from the main lot off Ridge Road. The **Red Rock Canyon Trail** is a flat 1.5-mile out-and-back through the heart of the canyon; combine it with the **Quarry Pass** and **Roundup** loops for a 3–4 mile day. Leashed dogs welcome on most trails — one of the few open spaces in town with that flexibility.

Climbers come here for the bolted sandstone walls. If you're just hiking, the best photos are from the small reservoir about 20 minutes in.
