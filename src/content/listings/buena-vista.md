**About 2 hours west** over Wilkerson Pass — a long but stunning drive. The **Arkansas Riverwalk** and **South Main** historic district are the walkable heart of town. Several outfitters run guided **whitewater rafting** day trips on the Brown's Canyon stretch (May–August).

For a mellower day, **Mount Princeton Hot Springs** is 20 minutes south and worth the soak. Pack swim gear and a towel either way.
