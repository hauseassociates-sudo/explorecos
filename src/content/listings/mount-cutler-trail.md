The trailhead is a small pullout partway up North Cheyenne Cañon Road — if the lot is full, keep driving and come back. The grade is steady but never steep, gaining about 500 feet to a broad summit ridge. On a clear afternoon you can see Kansas.

Connect to the **Muscoco Trail** for another 0.6 miles of climbing and an even quieter overlook.
