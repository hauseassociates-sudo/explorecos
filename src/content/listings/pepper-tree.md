Perched on a hillside overlooking downtown — the night view is the secret weapon. The signature **Pepper Steak** is prepared **tableside on a flame cart**, as is the **Bananas Foster** finish.

Jacket is not required but won't feel out of place. **Reservations essential**, especially on weekends. The wine list runs deep — the sommelier knows it cold.
