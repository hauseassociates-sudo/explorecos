About **a 1-hour drive south** of Colorado Springs through Cañon City. The bridge itself is the headline — walking across is included with admission, and looking through the wood planks at the river that far below is unforgettable.

Bundle the **Cloudscraper Zip Line** or **Royal Rush Skycoaster** for an adrenaline day. The whole park is wheelchair-accessible. Combine with a stop at **Skyline Drive** in Cañon City on the way back.
