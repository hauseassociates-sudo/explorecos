Start at **Acacia Park** (corner of Tejon and Platte) and walk south — you'll pass Poor Richard's, the Pikes Peak Center, a dozen lunch spots, and the Olympic Museum before you know it. Free two-hour street parking, or use the City Auditorium garage.

Friday and Saturday nights the whole strip comes alive. Summer Saturday mornings there's often a **farmers' market** near Acacia Park.
