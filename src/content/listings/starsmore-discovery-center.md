Small and delightful — plan 30–45 minutes inside, then walk out the back door to the **Columbine Trail** or drive up to Helen Hunt Falls a few minutes further into the canyon. Free parking, usually uncrowded, and an easy cool-off spot when the canyon road gets sunny.
