Bring swimsuits and towels — kids go straight through the fountain's misting arches on warm days and never come back dry. Right across from the U.S. Olympic & Paralympic Museum, so pair the two for a classic downtown afternoon.

The **Pikes Peak Greenway Trail** runs directly through the park — great for a stroller walk or bike ride along the creek.
