North of the city in the Black Forest — about 20 minutes from downtown and noticeably greener and cooler. The **Aspen Meadows Loop** (1.8 mi) is the classic family hike, and **Fallen Timbers Pond** is a short out-and-back the kids love.

Easy access to bathrooms and picnic areas. A great rainy-summer-day backup since the canopy keeps you mostly dry.
