Different from the **Bear Creek Nature Center** which sits inside it — the regional park has the wider trail network and large open spaces. The **Bear Creek Garden Association** demonstration gardens are tucked into the south side and worth a slow walk.

Off-leash dog area on the east end, archery range on the west. The **Bear Creek Greenway Trail** connects to Stratton Open Space if you want a longer route.
