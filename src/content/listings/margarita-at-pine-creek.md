One of those places visitors miss because it doesn't look like a restaurant from the road — keep going up the gravel drive. The **patio under the cottonwoods** is one of the loveliest dining spaces in the city.

The **five-course prix fixe** is the move on weekend evenings. The **Sunday brunch** with a build-your-own bloody bar is a local rite. Reserve at least a few days out in summer.
