The **Section 16 Loop** is one of the more honest workouts in town — about **1,400 ft of climb in 2 miles**, then a long ridge with views, then a mellow return through Bear Creek Canyon. Connects to **Palmer Trail** for a longer day.

Park at the **Section 16 / Intemann trailhead** at the corner of 26th and Gold Camp Road. Heavy mountain-bike traffic on weekend mornings — go counterclockwise to avoid most of it.
