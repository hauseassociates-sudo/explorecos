You don't need to be a guest to enjoy it. Walk the lake loop, grab cocktails at the **Tavern** or afternoon tea in the lobby, or book a lunch at **Natural Epicurean** on the lawn. The floral arrangements alone are worth the visit.

For a special-occasion dinner, **Penrose Room** is the only Forbes Five-Star restaurant in Colorado — reservations well ahead.
