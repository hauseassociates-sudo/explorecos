Park at the **free shuttle lot** at the east end of town and ride in — Manitou Avenue parking is rough on summer weekends. Don't miss **Patsy's Candies** (if they have seasonal flavors in), the **Penny Arcade** (hand-cranked games from the 1920s), and any of the mineral-springs fountains you pass — they're free and drinkable.

Pair with breakfast at Adam's Mountain Cafe or dinner at Mona Lisa Fondue for a full day in town.
