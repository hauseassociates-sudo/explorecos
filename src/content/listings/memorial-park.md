Most days you'll find paddleboarders on **Prospect Lake**, kids on the beach, and a steady rotation of pickup soccer on the fields. The **Honor Bell** memorial and the **Sertich Ice Arena** are part of the same complex.

In summer the city hosts free concerts and the **Labor Day Lift Off** balloon festival staging is right here. It's a good "rest day" stop — easy to combine with brunch or a stroll to nearby breweries.
