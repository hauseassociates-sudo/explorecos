Just off I-25 on the north side, easy to combine with a visit to the **Air Force Academy**. The exhibits cover the eight rodeo events with video and gear from inductees, plus animal hall-of-famers (yes, bucking horses get inducted).

Outdoor exhibits include a **bronze of "Rodeo's Greatest Hits"** and a paddock with longhorns. About an hour visit, longer if you have a rodeo fan in the family.
