Right at the **west end of US-24** as you head toward Manitou — easy to add to a trip out to Garden of the Gods. The buildings inside are real: original 1880s structures relocated and reassembled into a complete walk-through town.

Kids can pan for "gold," see the player piano work, and try out the old-time photo booth. About 45 minutes to an hour.
