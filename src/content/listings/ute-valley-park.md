The signed loop from the Vindicator Drive trailhead is a forgiving ~2 miles with a handful of short climbs and sweeping views of the Rampart Range. Add the **Pinon Ridge** spur for a quiet overlook most visitors miss.

Shared with mountain bikers — step aside on blind corners. Trails drain fast after rain, making this a reliable wet-day option.
