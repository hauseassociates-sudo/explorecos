Founded in 1953, burned in the 2012 Waldo Canyon Fire, and **fully rebuilt and reopened** — the new ranch sits on the same land and the show is the same as it was. The **Flying W Wranglers** are a real Grammy-nominated cowboy band; they're not pretending.

Reservations are essential, especially summer weekends. Show up early to wander the **mock western town** before dinner. Casual dress. Bring a jacket — the dining is open-air.
