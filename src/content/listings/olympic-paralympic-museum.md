Book timed-entry tickets online — walk-ups are often turned away on weekends. Allow **2 to 2.5 hours** for the full experience; the interactive sports challenges are the kids' highlight and the adults' surprise. Wheelchair-accessible throughout — designed from the ground up to be the most accessible museum in the U.S.

Combine with lunch downtown and a stroll along America the Beautiful Park, directly across the street.
