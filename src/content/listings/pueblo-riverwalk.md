**About 45 minutes south** down I-25. The riverwalk itself is free — paved paths along the channel with bridges, public art, and waterfalls. Several restaurants line it; **Brues Alehouse** has the best riverside patio.

Combine with a trip to **Lake Pueblo State Park** for boating or a stop at the **Pueblo Chile Festival** (late September) if your timing works. **Bishop Castle** is also drivable from here on a full day out.
