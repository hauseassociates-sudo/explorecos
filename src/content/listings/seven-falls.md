Operated by The Broadmoor. Access is via a shuttle from the main lot — you can't drive in. Climb the **224-step staircase** (it's harder than it sounds at elevation) or take the in-canyon elevator if you prefer. At the top, the **Inspiration Point Trail** adds another 15–20 minutes for a sweeping overlook.

Evening "restaurant + illuminated falls" tickets are a lovely date-night option in summer.
