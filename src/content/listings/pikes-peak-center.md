Parking is at the adjacent garage or street — arrive 30 minutes early on big-show nights. The **Pikes Peak Center app** for mobile tickets is the easy way to get in. Dress code is casual; this isn't a black-tie room.

Pair with dinner a few blocks away — Shuga's or The Famous are both under a 10-minute walk.
