If you came to Colorado Springs to work for a few mornings, this is the spot — strong wifi, plenty of outlets, and **espresso that actually shows you what they're about**. The **flat white** and **cortado** are the staples.

Pastries rotate from local bakeries. Order at the bar; baristas are friendly without being chatty. Two locations now (downtown and northside) but the original on Nevada has the best vibe.
