A useful rainy-day or beat-the-altitude-headache option — air conditioning, food, and walking distance for an entire afternoon. The **Cinemark 14** is a working 1990s-style mall theater that locals still go to.

Anchors are **Dillard's, JCPenney, Macy's**. About 15 minutes from downtown. Probably not the reason you came to COS, but a useful backstop.
