A **15-minute drive** up Highway 24 toward Pikes Peak. The park has been operating since 1956 and it shows in the best way — small wooden rides, a magic carpet slide, and the highlight: **Santa himself**, available year-round to chat with kids.

Best for ages 2–10. Plan 2–3 hours. Combine with a drive up **Pikes Peak Highway** if your kids will tolerate it (the cog railway is a more relaxed alternative).
